Our mission

The Australian Literacy & Numeracy Foundation is here to create a more inclusive Australia, unlocking potential through the power of literacy and learning.

  • Founded 1999
  • 8 national programs across Australia
  • Over 17,000 children reached in 2023

Everyone’s got a story to tell. We believe they should be free to tell it with confidence.

Our programs

Whether it’s through books for kids, championing strong literacy and educational equality in the vital prior-to-school years, supporting refugees or helping First Nations peoples keep their languages and cultures alive, we are driven to inspire literacy and learning every day.

Our impact

The Australian Literacy and Numeracy Foundation has been transforming literacy and educational outcomes across the nation for over 25 years, creating systemic, sustainable and community-led change.

Breaking the cycle of transgenerational illiteracy and disadvantage in marginalised communities across Australia.

Our partners and champions.

Literacy is one of the most challenging issues in Australia today, and requires sharing and exchanging knowledge, and new ways of thinking, acting, and partnering for change.
